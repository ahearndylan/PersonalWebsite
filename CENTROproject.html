Centro Non-Profit Project

Project Overview

This project focuses on improving the digital presence of Centro, a non-profit organization serving the Worcester community. By upgrading their website with modern UI/UX, improved functionality, and mobile-friendly features, we aim to increase user engagement and provide a better experience for those looking to get involved with the organization.

Project Status: Currently under development. Key features and improvements are being implemented to enhance the user experience.

About Centro

Established in 1977, Centro is the largest minority-led, community-based, multiservice, multicultural, and multilingual nonprofit organization in Central Massachusetts. The organizationâ€™s mission is to help individuals and families achieve self-sufficiency while promoting social responsibility and fostering cultural identity.

Centro provides a wide range of services, including health, behavioral, and social services, to over 24,000 people annually from diverse backgrounds, including Latino, African, Asian, and Middle Eastern communities.

Project Goals

The Centro Non-Profit project aims to:

  • Redesign the existing website with a focus on improved user experience and accessibility.
  • Ensure the website is fully responsive, providing a seamless experience across devices, especially mobile phones.
  • Incorporate new features, such as online donation options and interactive forms for volunteers and community members to engage with Centro more easily.

Anticipated Outcomes

The anticipated outcomes of this project include:

  • Enhancing the community's ability to access services provided by Centro through a modern and user-friendly website.
  • Increasing engagement with volunteers, donors, and community members via an improved digital platform.
  • Providing the Centro team with tools to manage and update their website more easily, ensuring sustainability and long-term usability.

Future Plans

As the project develops, future enhancements may include:

  • Implementing a community forum where users can share experiences, ask questions, and provide feedback.
  • Creating a system for Centro staff to update content and add events in real-time, without needing external assistance.
  • Further integration with social media platforms for better outreach and engagement.
