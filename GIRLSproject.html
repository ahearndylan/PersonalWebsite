About Girls Inc. of Worcester

Girls Inc. of Worcester, founded in 1916, is the oldest girls-only, facility-based youth organization in Central Massachusetts. Their mission is to inspire girls to be strong (healthy), smart (educated), and bold (independent) by equipping them with the tools needed to overcome social, economic, and gender-based barriers.

The organization reaches over 1,400 girls annually, offering research-based programming that encourages girls to set and achieve goals, resist peer pressure, pursue nontraditional fields like STEM, and see college as attainable. Their work is rooted in providing real solutions to the unique challenges that girls face, making them an essential resource for empowering young women in the community.

Project Goals

This project aims to:

  • Redesign the existing Girls Inc. of Worcester website to be more modern, accessible, and engaging.
  • Ensure the site is mobile-friendly, with a responsive design that adapts to different devices.
  • Add features such as volunteer sign-ups, program information, and donation options to support the organization's growth and outreach.

Anticipated Outcomes

The anticipated outcomes of this project include:

  • Increasing visibility and engagement for Girls Inc. through a modernized website.
  • Enhancing the ability of Girls Inc. to attract new volunteers, donors, and community members.
  • Providing a seamless user experience for those looking to support or participate in the organization's programs.

Future Plans

Future improvements and expansion plans include:

  • Incorporating an interactive calendar for events and programs.
  • Building an online community platform for young women to connect, learn, and share experiences.
  • Adding tools for staff to easily manage and update content, ensuring long-term sustainability for the site.
