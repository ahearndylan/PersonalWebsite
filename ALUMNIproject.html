Alumni App Project

Project Overview

The Alumni App is being developed to connect Clark University students and alumni. Students can request assistance with career-related activities, such as resume reviews, mock interviews, and mentorship opportunities, while alumni can choose which requests to respond to based on their expertise and job roles.

The goal is to foster meaningful professional connections that strengthen the Clark community and enrich students' career development.

Project Goals

The Alumni App aims to:

  • Provide a platform for mentorship and career development for Clark University students.
  • Facilitate easy access for alumni to give back to the Clark community through their professional expertise.
  • Create a stronger connection between alumni and current students, enhancing the overall Clark network.

Funding Request

We are requesting $250 to cover two years of hosting costs for the Alumni App. This amount will ensure that the app remains functional and accessible to students and alumni. Hosting will be done on DigitalOcean, providing secure access to all users.

Upcoming Discussion: A meeting is planned with the Dean of the College to discuss funding options and ensure the long-term sustainability of the platform. This funding will be essential for securing the necessary resources to complete the project and support its ongoing use by the Clark community.

Anticipated Outcomes

The anticipated outcomes of this project include:

  • Providing students with personalized career mentorship and advice from alumni.
  • Strengthening the professional network between Clark alumni and current students.
  • Offering real-world software development experience for Clark computer science students.

Future Plans

Once the project is complete, the Alumni App will be hosted on DigitalOcean for secure access by the Clark community. Potential future features include:

  • Integration with professional social media platforms to enhance alumni engagement.
  • Additional career development resources for students.
